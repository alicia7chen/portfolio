Internship Work

During the summer of 2021 I interned at Comcast as a UX Research Analyst Intern. Through this internship I was exposed to both sides of qualitative and quantitative UX methods such as conducting user interviews via userzoom go, data analysis using R and RStudio, survey programming with Qualtrics, retrieving existing and relevant research from the Looker database to inform current and future research projects and general workshopping and brainstorming through Miro and Freehand.

  • Xfinity Connected Living: drafted and revised survey to reduce cognitive load, programmed survey in Qualtrics, facilitated customer interviews via userzoom go, created 4 user personas based on survey and interview data
  • Xfinity Privacy: data analysis using R and Excel macros on survey data to determine customer trust levels of chat bots, information customers are comfortable/not comfortable sharing, and preferred authentication methods
  • Xfinity Sports Segmentation: used k means clustering to create segments of customers based on similar traits and behaviors in relation to sports content consuption
